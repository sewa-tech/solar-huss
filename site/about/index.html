In terms of using solar energy in various ways, it has been well established that water heating technology using a solar water heater has been around in Nepal since few decades, and its popularity is increasing widely. A solar water heater heats up water from 500C to 850C depending on the weather condition and its structure. Not requiring special care; Its features such as automatic operation after installation, direct saving of time and money spent electricity and gas makes a solar water heater economical and efficient solution for daily hot water needs.

WORK FEATURES

Inner boiler tank

Special boiler tanks coated using dip galvanized process are available. Due to the fact that the upper and lower wheels of the drum have been pressure tested by shaping it into a dome, the internal parts of the solar boiler tanks are completely strong and trustworthy.

Electric heating system

Our solar water heater set can be equipped with an automatic electric heating system including an indicator, assuring availability of hot water even during winters or cloudy days. This system is optional and is available as per requirement.

Insulation

High quality Vermin Proof Fiber Wool is used keeping water inside the boiler tanks warm for longer period of time.

Outer Shell

Outer cover with aluminum dome made from thick sheets protects the boiler tanks from physical damage and weathering. Also, these colorful outer shell looks attractive.

Solar Panels

High quality special black paint is applied on solar panel and thick aluminum sheets with durable panel box and stand are used during manufacturing. These panel comes with colorful frames making them attractive and strong.

Rubber Seal

Since a special rubber seal is placed between the solar panel and the glass, it can get rid of the hassle of changing the rubber seal and painting it every year.

OURPROCESS

Important tips to consider before installation

OUR PROCESS
01
Location for solar heater and the hot water pipeline installation should be consulted with skilled technician while building the house.
02
It is advisable to keep the hot water pipe as short as possible
03
Hot water pipe should be properly insulated. Covering it with insulation tube within thin polythene pipe cover makes them durable.
04
Location for solar set placement should be out in open space facing South and should not be shaded. At least from 11:00 am to 4:00 pm, it is important to get good sunlight.
05
Cold water tank should be at least 5 1/2 feet above the solar water heater set. Height of 6 feet or above is even better.
06
In order to use a pressure pump, the manufacturer should be informed in advance and the pressure tested solar set should be installed accordingly.
07
Get complete information about the build quality and liter capacity of solar before installing the solar set.
08
Be aware that most imported vacuum tube water heater sets lack maintenance facilities/ after installation services.
